## üìÑ Baptism and Confirmation Record

### Record and Certificate Process

Before a child who is a member of record is interviewed for baptism:

1. **Clerk** uses LCR to prepare a **Baptism and Confirmation Form**
2. **Bishop or assigned counselor** conducts the interview and signs the form
3. After baptism and confirmation, a **clerk** uses this form to update the child's membership record in LCR
4. After the membership record is created, a **clerk** prepares a **Baptism and Confirmation Certificate**
5. Certificate is signed by the **bishop** and given to the person

## üéµ Baptismal Service Program

[Download Word Template](Baptismal_Service_Program.docx) - Editable Word document

A baptismal service should be **simple, brief, and spiritually uplifting**. 

### Recommended Program Order

1. **Prelude music**
2. **Brief welcome** from the brother conducting the service
3. **Opening hymn and prayer**
4. **One or two short messages** on gospel subjects, such as:
   - Baptism
   - The gift of the Holy Ghost
5. **Musical selection**
6. **The baptism**
7. **Time of reverence** while participants change into dry clothes
   - Hymns or Primary songs may be played or sung during this time
8. **The confirmation** of 8-year-old members of record; the confirmation of converts (see 18.8)
9. **Bearing of testimonies** by new converts, if desired
10. **Closing hymn and prayer**
11. **Postlude music**
